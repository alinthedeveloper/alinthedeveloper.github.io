My Music:

Dreamin'

Brief Breakdown:

Dreamin' combines existential inquiry with pop culture references, spiritual symbolism, and a quest for personal and universal understanding. Throughout the song, I encourage listeners to embark on their own journey of self-discovery, embracing the dreams and visions that propel us forward in search of meaning and connection.

Metaphorfisis (album)

Brief Breakdown:

Metaphorfisis is a journey of introspection, self-discovery, and change, woven into the fabric of alternative pop / hip-hop. The project's main "thesis statement" is found in the spoken word poem I perform in breakingchains:

In the beginning, there was nothing but darkness

A hazy empty void

But then

A spark

A spark of hope so bright

That it burned through the night

The pain still hurt the same

But without the pain, there was no flame

An ember

that when fostered started to grow

Into a fire

A fire

A fire in my soul

The choir pierced through the dark

It brought power to the spark

So I stepped out of line

And let the music out of my heart

We Are Stardust

Brief Breakdown:

We Are Stardust invites listeners on a celestial journey of love and transcendence, blending elements of romance, wonder, and cosmic exploration. The song paints a dreamy landscape where lovers lay beneath the stars, basking in the beauty of the universe and their own connection. With vivid imagery and poetic lyricism, it captures the ineffable magic of shared moments, where time seems to stand still and the boundaries between earthly and celestial realms blur. I wanted the song to be a mesmerizing ode to love, unity, and the timeless allure of the cosmos.

Life Is Good

Brief Breakdown:

Life Is Good navigates the complexities of love and longing, juxtaposing the joys of existence with the ache of separation. Through heartfelt lyricism and introspective storytelling, the song reflects on the bittersweet nostalgia of past moments shared with a loved one. It explores themes of regret, yearning, and the universal desire for connection, inviting listeners to contemplate the fleeting nature of happiness and the enduring power of love. With its candid portrayal of emotional vulnerability and raw honesty, "Life Is Good" captures the essence of human experience and the ever-present longing for what once was.

Distraction

Brief Breakdown:

Distraction delves into the complexities of coping mechanisms and self-preservation in the face of inner turmoil. Through dynamic lyricism and a blend of introspection and bravado, the song explores the ways in which individuals navigate pain and uncertainty by immersing themselves in distractions. It juxtaposes moments of vulnerability and introspection with bursts of confidence and defiance, reflecting the internal struggle to find solace and purpose amidst chaos.

Rainstorm

Brief Breakdown:

Rainstorm captures the essence of resilience and liberation amidst adversity, symbolized by dancing in the midst of a storm. The song evokes a sense of empowerment and freedom, as we find solace and purpose in the face of challenges. Through evocative imagery and emotive lyricism, the song explores themes of self-discovery, letting go of toxic relationships, and embracing newfound independence.

posted pretty.

Brief Breakdown:

Posted pretty paints a picture of infatuation and attraction, capturing the essence of desire through rhythmic lyricism and vibrant imagery. The song paints a picture of admiration for someone captivating, expressed through metaphors of swagger and allure. With its infectious rhythm and catchy hooks, the song celebrates the intoxicating allure of newfound connections and the excitement of exploring mutual attraction.
