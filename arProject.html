AR Top Down Shooter

Gallery

Overview:

This project challenged our team to create an immersive gaming experience utilizing AR technology. We opted to develop a unique twist on the traditional top-down shooter genre, where one player controls the protagonist using a computer keyboard and mouse, while another player commands enemies and bosses using specially designed cards.

Goals:

The primary goal of the project was to leverage AR technology to create an interactive and engaging gaming experience that blends physical and digital elements seamlessly. By incorporating innovative gameplay mechanics and real-world interaction through the use of cards, we aimed to push the boundaries of traditional gaming experiences and explore new possibilities in AR game design.

Insights & Skills:

Throughout the project, I gained valuable insights into the collaborative workflow of developing a larger-scale project within a team and under time constraints. Working alongside teammates, I learned how to effectively communicate and coordinate tasks, manage resources, and navigate the complexities of collaborative game development.

Key Learnings:

One of the key takeaways from this project was the importance of effective teamwork and communication in achieving project goals. By collaborating with teammates with diverse skills and expertise, I gained a deeper understanding of the iterative development process and the significance of clear communication and coordination in delivering a cohesive and polished product.

Why I took the project:

The project presented an exciting opportunity to explore the intersection of AR technology and game design. I was drawn to the project by the prospect of pushing the boundaries of traditional gaming experiences and experimenting with innovative gameplay mechanics. Additionally, the opportunity to contribute to various aspects of the game, including sound design and sprite creation, made the project a rewarding and enjoyable endeavor.
